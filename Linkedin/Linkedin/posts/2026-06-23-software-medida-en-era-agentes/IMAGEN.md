# Imagen

## Objetivo visual

Mostrar que el valor esta en conectar herramientas, datos y procesos, no en una unica aplicacion aislada.

## Tipo de imagen

Imagen generada conceptual/profesional.

## Prompt o instrucciones

Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: LinkedIn post image, square 1200x1200
Primary request: A clean professional business-tech visual showing multiple abstract software tools connected into one coherent workflow.
Scene/backdrop: Modern desk or workspace with screens showing generic app panels, spreadsheets, messages, and process nodes, without readable text.
Subject: A central integration flow connecting several abstract business systems, with a subtle AI automation layer assisting the connections.
Style/medium: Photorealistic editorial image with light digital overlay.
Composition/framing: Balanced square image, central connected workflow, no clutter.
Lighting/mood: Bright, practical, trustworthy.
Color palette: Neutral whites and grays with accents in teal, green, and blue.
Constraints: No logos, no brand names, no readable text, no fake numbers, no humanoid robots, no dark sci-fi style.

## Formato sugerido

1200 x 1200, cuadrada.

## Notas de privacidad

No usar marcas reales ni pantallas con datos legibles.
