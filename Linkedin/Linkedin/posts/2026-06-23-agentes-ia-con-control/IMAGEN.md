# Imagen

## Objetivo visual

Representar agentes de IA ayudando a automatizar flujos de trabajo, con una persona manteniendo supervision y control.

## Tipo de imagen

Imagen generada, estilo editorial profesional para LinkedIn.

## Prompt o instrucciones

Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: LinkedIn post image, square 1200x1200
Primary request: Create a professional, modern visual about AI agents helping businesses automate repetitive workflows while humans keep approval and control.
Scene/backdrop: Bright contemporary office workspace with a laptop showing abstract workflow nodes, calendars, documents, and business process cards, all without readable text.
Subject: A human professional reviewing an automation flow on a laptop/tablet, with subtle translucent AI assistant elements represented as clean connected nodes, not a robot.
Style/medium: Photorealistic editorial business image with tasteful digital overlays.
Composition/framing: Square composition, eye-level, human and device in foreground, workflow visualization visible but not crowded, generous clean space.
Lighting/mood: Natural daylight, calm, practical, trustworthy, not futuristic sci-fi.
Color palette: Neutral office tones with restrained accents in teal, blue, and warm white.
Constraints: No logos, no brand names, no readable text, no fake dashboard numbers, no humanoid robots, no exaggerated neon, no dark cyberpunk mood, no watermarks.

## Formato sugerido

1200 x 1200, cuadrada. Conviene para LinkedIn porque funciona bien en feed y no depende de texto incrustado.

## Notas de privacidad

No contiene marcas, clientes, datos reales ni interfaces legibles.
