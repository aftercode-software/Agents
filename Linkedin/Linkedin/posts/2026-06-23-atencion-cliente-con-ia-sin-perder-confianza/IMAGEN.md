# Imagen

## Objetivo visual

Representar soporte asistido por IA con supervision humana y foco en confianza.

## Tipo de imagen

Imagen generada profesional.

## Prompt o instrucciones

Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: LinkedIn post image, square 1200x1200
Primary request: Create a professional image about AI-assisted customer support that keeps a human in control.
Scene/backdrop: Clean customer support workspace with a professional reviewing abstract conversation cards and support workflow indicators, no readable text.
Subject: Human support specialist at a desk with subtle AI assistance represented as organized, connected conversation bubbles and status nodes.
Style/medium: Photorealistic editorial business image.
Composition/framing: Square, human-centered, calm and practical, not too futuristic.
Lighting/mood: Warm daylight, trustworthy, service-oriented.
Color palette: Neutral workspace tones with accents in teal, blue, and soft green.
Constraints: No logos, no brand names, no readable chat messages, no fake customer data, no robots, no dark cyberpunk mood.

## Formato sugerido

1200 x 1200, cuadrada.

## Notas de privacidad

Evitar interfaces legibles, nombres de clientes y datos personales.
